# Bob Sumerel Tire Co. Reduces Their Risk Exposure by Partnering with Foley

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/bob-sumerel](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/bob-sumerel)

---

## Company Overview

Bob Sumerel Tire Co. is a multi-location tire and automotive service company managing a fleet of DOT-regulated drivers across **24 locations**. The company partnered with Foley to simplify compliance and reduce risk exposure across its distributed operation.

---

## Key Contact

**Bob Hickey** – (Customer spokesperson, video testimonial)

---

## The Challenge

- Managing DOT compliance across 24 separate locations with consistency
- Significant risk exposure from driver monitoring gaps
- Rising insurance costs and difficulty justifying current rates to insurers
- Staying current with industry regulatory changes

---

## The Foley Solution

- **Easy-to-use software platform** accessible and consistent across all 24 locations
- **Simplified DOT Compliance** — centralized management replacing fragmented processes
- **Driver monitoring products** — continuous visibility into driver records and risk signals
- **Insurance partnership leverage** — Foley's platform used directly in conversations with insurers to demonstrate compliance posture
- **Dedicated Customer Success Manager** — ongoing support and proactive communication on industry changes

---

## Results

- **Significantly reduced risk exposure** through driver monitoring
- **Maintained current insurance rates** by leveraging Foley's compliance data in insurance negotiations
- Always informed on regulatory changes — no surprises
- Consistent compliance management across all 24 locations
- Strong ongoing relationship with a dedicated CSM
