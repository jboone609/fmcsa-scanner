## Company Overview

Clutter is a high-tech storage and moving company operating across multiple locations. As a tech-forward business managing a fleet of DOT-regulated drivers, Clutter needed a compliance platform that matched its modern infrastructure — not a legacy system built on outdated technology.

## The Challenge

- Previous compliance vendor's software **only worked with Internet Explorer** — a significant technology barrier for a modern tech company
- Creating driver qualification files was **too slow** and cumbersome in the legacy system
- Managing compliance across **multiple locations** was a nightmare with the incumbent tool
- Platform limitations were creating operational drag and compliance risk

---

## The Foley Solution

- Migration to the **Foley Platform** — modern, multi-browser, multi-location capable
- Streamlined driver file creation — faster and more intuitive than the legacy system
- Multi-location compliance management built into the platform architecture
- Scalable infrastructure that fits a tech-first company's expectations

## Results

- Eliminated Internet Explorer dependency — platform works on modern browsers
- **Driver file creation significantly faster** than the previous system
- Multi-location compliance is now **manageable and consistent**
- Head of Compliance reports high satisfaction with platform efficiency
