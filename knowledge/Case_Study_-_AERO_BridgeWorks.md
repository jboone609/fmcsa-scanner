# AERO BridgeWorks Saves $40,000+ Per Year with Foley's Automated Solution

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/aero-bridgeworks-saves-with-foleys-automation](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/aero-bridgeworks-saves-with-foleys-automation)

---

## Company Overview

AERO BridgeWorks is a construction and infrastructure services company operating a fleet of DOT-regulated drivers. Before Foley, they managed compliance using paper files spread across multiple locations — a system that was costly, inefficient, and audit-risky.

---

## The Challenge

- **Paper-based driver files** — reviewing a single driver file required checking **3–4 separate physical locations**
- Inefficient compliance management consuming significant staff time and resources
- No centralized system for onboarding new drivers or tracking ongoing compliance
- Difficulty staying ahead of compliance deadlines and regulatory requirements

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley created a **streamlined, digital compliance system**:

- Centralized driver file management — all documents in one place
- Automated onboarding workflow for new drivers
- Ongoing compliance tracking to stay ahead of deadlines
- Eliminated the need to search across multiple physical file locations

---

## Results

- **$40,000+ saved per year** through automation and process efficiency
- Onboarding new drivers is now fast and consistent
- Compliance management is proactive, not reactive
- Eliminated the multi-location paper file system entirely
