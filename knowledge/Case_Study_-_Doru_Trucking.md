# How Doru Trucking Uses Foley's MVR Monitoring to Maintain Its Hard-Won Safety Rating

## Company Overview

Doru Trucking is a carrier whose safety rating was previously downgraded to **Conditional** — a serious FMCSA designation that signals compliance deficiencies and increases audit risk. The company has since worked to restore and maintain its safety standing using Foley's MVR Monitoring solution.

---

## The Challenge

- Safety rating had been **downgraded to Conditional** by the FMCSA — a significant red flag that can impact insurance rates, shipper relationships, and ongoing audit exposure
- Needed continuous visibility into driver record changes to prevent future violations
- Required a proactive approach to driver risk management to restore and protect the company's safety rating
- Annual MVR pulls were not sufficient — issues can arise between review cycles

---

## The Foley Solution

- **Foley MVR Monitoring** — continuous, real-time monitoring of driver Motor Vehicle Records
- Instant alerts when driver records change (new violations, license suspensions, etc.)
- Proactive risk management rather than reactive annual reviews
- Compliance posture strengthened to support safety rating recovery and maintenance

---

## Results

- Successfully working to **maintain and protect the restored safety rating**
- Continuous MVR visibility prevents surprises between annual review cycles
- Driver risk managed proactively — issues addressed before they escalate
- Stronger compliance posture supporting improved FMCSA standing

---

## Key Concept: MVR Monitoring vs. Annual MVR Pull

| Approach | Frequency | Risk Level |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| Annual MVR Pull | Once per year | High — issues can go undetected for months |
| Foley MVR Monitoring | Continuous / Real-time | Low — alerts triggered immediately on record changes |
