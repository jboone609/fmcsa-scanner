# How Foley Helped DNC Logistics Cut Driver File Management Time by 50%

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/how-foley-helped-dnc-logistics-cut-driver-file-management-time-by-50-percent](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/how-foley-helped-dnc-logistics-cut-driver-file-management-time-by-50-percent)

---

## Company Overview

DNC Logistics was founded in 2002 and operated for years using a combination of **paper files and Outlook calendar alerts** to manage driver compliance. As the company grew, the limitations of that approach became increasingly apparent — and costly.

---

## The Challenge

- Driver file management built on **paper records and manual Outlook calendar reminders**
- High risk of missed deadlines and compliance gaps as driver count grew
- Time-consuming, error-prone manual process consuming significant administrative resources
- No centralized system — compliance lived in email calendars and physical folders

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley replaced DNC's manual system with a digital, automated compliance platform:

- **Centralized digital driver files** — all records in one place, accessible anytime
- **Automated deadline tracking** — replaced manual Outlook calendar alerts
- Streamlined compliance management reducing the burden on administrative staff

---

## Results

- **50% reduction in driver file management time**
- Eliminated manual calendar-based tracking and the risk of missed compliance deadlines
- Compliance management transitioned from reactive (calendar reminders) to proactive (automated alerts)
- Administrative resources freed up for higher-value work
