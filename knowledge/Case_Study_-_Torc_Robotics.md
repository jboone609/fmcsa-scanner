## Company Overview

**Torc Robotics** is the first company to develop a profitable, scalable, commercialized **Level 4 autonomous truck** solution for over-the-road trucking. Despite the autonomous nature of the technology, DOT regulations still require full driver compliance for any operator in the cab — making DOT compliance a non-negotiable requirement even at the frontier of autonomous transport.

---

## The Challenge

- Unique business model — autonomous trucks still require DOT-compliant human operators in the cab
- Standard compliance solutions were not designed for Torc's specialized, technology-forward operation
- Needed a compliance partner that could build a **custom solution** rather than force a generic fit
- Managing driver qualification files across a rapidly evolving, high-tech fleet environment

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley developed a **custom compliance stack** specifically for Torc Robotics' business model:

- Tailored software and service solutions designed around autonomous trucking operations
- Digital driver file management — automated and centralized
- Audit-ready compliance infrastructure for a non-standard fleet operation
- Ongoing compliance management keeping pace with Torc's innovation

---

## Results

- **~50% reduction** in time spent managing driver files
- **Audit-ready every day** — compliance maintained continuously, not just before audits
- Custom solution that fits Torc's unique business model — not a generic off-the-shelf product
- Compliance infrastructure that scales with Torc's commercial growth
