# PKS Concrete Pumping Services Boosts Onboarding Efficiency by 75% with Foley

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/pks-concrete](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/pks-concrete)

---

## Company Overview

PKS Concrete Pumping Services is a concrete pumping company operating a fleet of DOT-regulated vehicles. Prior to Foley, compliance and driver onboarding were managed manually — a process that regularly resulted in missed tasks, delays, and regulatory exposure.

---

## The Challenge

- Compliance managed **entirely manually** — no automated tracking or alerts
- Overlooked compliance tasks due to the volume and complexity of requirements
- Risk of fines and violations from missed deadlines
- Driver onboarding was slow and inconsistently executed
- Multiple compliance requirements (drug testing, physicals, MVRs, file management) tracked separately with no unified system

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley's platform automated and centralized PKS Concrete's compliance workflow:

- **Drug and Alcohol Testing** — automated program management
- **Driver File Management** — centralized DQ files with deadline tracking
- **DOT Physicals** — scheduling and record management
- **MVR Monitoring** — continuous driver record monitoring
- **Foley Dash** — unified platform ensuring all compliance steps completed on time

---

## Results

- **75% improvement in onboarding efficiency**
- All compliance steps completed by deadline — **no fines or penalties** from missed requirements
- Eliminated the manual tracking that caused overlooked tasks
- Unified compliance management across all requirements in one platform
