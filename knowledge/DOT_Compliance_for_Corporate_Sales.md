# Driver Qualification File (DQF)

A DOT-mandated digital folder that proves a driver is eligible to
operate a CMV.

Foley automates collection, reminders, and digital storage for every
item below.

## What's in the File

### DOT-Compliant Application

Must include: - 10-year job history (for driving roles) - Explanation of
gaps \> 1 month - Traffic violations - Accident history - License
information - Signed certification

### Motor Vehicle Record (MVR)

-   Pulled from all states the driver held a license in the past 3
    years\
-   Must be reviewed at hire and annually

### Safety Performance History (SPH)

Verification of previous DOT-regulated employers (past 3 years),
including: - Dates of employment\
- Accidents\
- Drug/alcohol testing violations

### Medical Examiner's Certificate

-   Valid medical card from a certified examiner\
-   Must be renewed every 2 years (or more frequently if required)

### Driver's License

-   Must be valid and appropriate class (CDL or non-CDL)

### Road Test Certificate or CDL

-   If no CDL, employer must administer and retain a road test
    certificate

### Annual Review + Certificate of Violations

-   Annual MVR review\
-   Signed list of traffic violations

### ELDT Certificate (if applicable)

-   Required for drivers who obtained a CDL after February 2022

# Drug & Alcohol Testing (DAT)

A federally mandated testing program for all CDL drivers operating
safety-sensitive vehicles.

Foley manages enrollment, scheduling, reporting, and recordkeeping.

## Requirements

### Pre-employment Drug Test

-   Required before driver can perform safety-sensitive functions

### Random Testing

-   50% of drivers per year for drugs\
-   10% per year for alcohol\
-   Must be scientifically random and unannounced

### Other Tests

-   Post-accident\
-   Reasonable Suspicion\
-   Return-to-Duty\
-   Follow-up

### MRO & DER

-   Results reviewed by a Medical Review Officer (MRO)\
-   Employers must designate a Designated Employer Representative (DER)

### Retention Requirements

-   Records must be kept 1--5 years depending on record type

# FMCSA Clearinghouse

A centralized database that tracks CDL drivers' drug & alcohol program
violations and test history.

Foley automates queries, stores consents, and handles reporting
workflows.

## Key Concepts

### Full Query

-   Required before hiring a CDL driver\
-   Driver must consent through their Clearinghouse account

### Limited Query

-   Required annually for all active CDL drivers\
-   Consent can be bulk/long-term

### Reporting

-   Employers must report:
    -   Positive test results\
    -   Refusals\
    -   Return-to-Duty completions

### Consequences

-   Drivers with unresolved violations cannot perform safety-sensitive
    functions

### Fines

-   Up to \$5,833 per driver for failing to run required queries

# Understanding Regulatory Thresholds

## CDL-Regulated Drivers (Full DOT Compliance Required)

**Vehicle Weight:** Over 26,001 lbs

**Requires:** - Driver Qualification File\
- Drug & Alcohol Testing Program\
- Clearinghouse Queries\
- Valid CDL License

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Non-CDL but DOT-Regulated Drivers

**Vehicle Weight:** 10,001 -- 26,000 lbs

**Requires:** - Driver Qualification File\
- Valid Standard License\
- Medical Certificate

**Not Required:** - Drug testing\
- Clearinghouse

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Non-Regulated Drivers

**Vehicle Weight:** Under 10,001 lbs

**Requires:** - No formal DOT compliance

**Optional:** - MVR checks for insurance or internal policy
