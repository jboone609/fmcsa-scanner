# How Foley Provides Arbor Pro of Virginia with Streamlined FMCSA Clearinghouse Help

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/how-foley-provides-arbor-pro-of-virginia-with-streamlined-fmcsa-clearinghouse-help](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/how-foley-provides-arbor-pro-of-virginia-with-streamlined-fmcsa-clearinghouse-help)

## Company Overview

Arbor Pro of Virginia is a tree service and arborist company whose **business manager** took on DOT compliance responsibilities with limited prior regulatory experience. Navigating the FMCSA Clearinghouse was an immediate challenge — and Foley was recommended by an FMCSA safety inspector as the solution.

---

## The Challenge

- Business manager was **new to DOT compliance** — unfamiliar with regulations and requirements
- FMCSA Clearinghouse was particularly confusing: mandatory pre-employment and annual queries, driver consent management, violation tracking
- Called FMCSA directly seeking guidance and was **referred to Foley** by an FMCSA safety inspector
- High stress around ensuring Clearinghouse compliance without internal expertise to lean on

---

## The Foley Solution

- **Automated FMCSA Clearinghouse management** — Foley handles queries, consent tracking, and violation monitoring
- Ongoing compliance monitoring so Arbor Pro stays current without manual tracking
- Expert support to answer questions and reduce the learning curve for non-compliance staff

---

## Results

- **Stress significantly reduced** — no longer manually navigating Clearinghouse requirements
- **Fully compliant** with FMCSA Clearinghouse mandates
- Business manager can focus on running the business rather than regulatory complexity
- Confident in ongoing compliance standing

---

## Notable Detail

Arbor Pro was referred to Foley **directly by an FMCSA safety inspector** — a powerful third-party endorsement of Foley's credibility in the compliance space.
