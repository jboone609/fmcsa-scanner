# Celebrating Town of Athol's 21 Years of Partnership with Foley

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/town-of-athol](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/town-of-athol)

---

## Company Overview

The Town of Athol is a municipal government entity whose **Department of Public Works (DPW)** operates a fleet of DOT-regulated vehicles and drivers. Their 21-year partnership with Foley is one of the longest-standing customer relationships in Foley's history.

---

## Key Contact

**Diana Cooley** – Town of Athol (Customer spokesperson, video testimonial)

---

## The Challenge

- Municipal fleet operations require ongoing DOT compliance management
- Managing Return to Duty and Post-Accident Drug Testing processes
- Understanding the impact of CSA Scores on compliance standing and audit risk
- Ensuring audit readiness across a government fleet operation

---

## The Foley Solution

Over a 21-year partnership, Foley has provided:

- **DOT Compliance Management** — continuous, year-over-year compliance support
- **Audit Risk Monitor** — clearer understanding of CSA Score impacts and audit exposure
- **Return to Duty & Post-Accident Drug Testing** — managed process for required testing events
- **Subject Matter Expertise** — access to compliance specialists and responsive customer support

---

## Results

- **21 years of continuous DOT compliance** — never faced an audit in that time
- Complete clarity on **CSA Score impacts** and what drives audit risk
- Efficient management of Return to Duty and Post-Accident testing requirements
- Long-term confidence in compliance standing for a public-sector fleet
