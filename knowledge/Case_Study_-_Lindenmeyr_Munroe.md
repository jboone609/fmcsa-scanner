# How Foley is Turning Skepticism into Confidence for Lindenmeyr Munroe

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/lindenmeyrmunroe](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/lindenmeyrmunroe)

---

## Company Overview

Lindenmeyr Munroe is a large paper and packaging distributor operating **45 locations** across the country. Managing DOT compliance for both drivers and warehouse employees across that many sites created significant coordination and visibility challenges.

---

## The Challenge

- Managing compliance across **45 separate locations** with inconsistent processes
- Ensuring the right data and alerts reached the right location managers
- Risk of compliance gaps going unnoticed due to decentralized operations
- Initial skepticism about switching to a new compliance platform

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley's platform delivers:

- **Real-time notifications** surfaced to the appropriate location — each site sees only what's relevant to them
- Proactive alert management so important compliance events are addressed before they become violations
- Centralized oversight with location-level granularity

---

## Results

- Turned initial skepticism into **confidence** in Foley's platform
- **Real time savings** across locations
- **Reduced compliance risk** through proactive, location-specific alert management
- Compliance team now ahead of issues rather than reacting to them
