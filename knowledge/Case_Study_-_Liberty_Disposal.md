# How Foley's Digital Platform Created the Perfect Partnership

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/how-foleys-digital-platform-created-the-perfect-partnership](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/how-foleys-digital-platform-created-the-perfect-partnership)

---

## Company Overview

Liberty Disposal is a waste management and disposal company that began its partnership with Foley in **2019**. They were searching for not just a compliance vendor, but a true compliance *partner* — a distinction that drove their decision to make a change from their previous provider.

---

## The Challenge

- Previous compliance provider was not meeting expectations — gaps discovered only after enrolling in Foley's Driver Qualification File program
- Operating with **paper-based driver files** — time-consuming, error-prone, and difficult to manage at scale
- Needed both the right compliance *solution* and the right compliance *service* — the previous vendor delivered neither adequately

---

## The Foley Solution

A full transition from paper to digital, with a customized platform approach:

- **Driver Qualification File Program** — enrollment immediately surfaced gaps left by the previous provider
- **Fully customized digital platform** — tailored to Liberty Disposal's specific compliance needs
- **Committed customer service** — ongoing support that made Liberty Disposal feel like a true partner, not just a customer

---

## Results

- Discovered and remediated compliance gaps left by the previous vendor
- Completed journey from **paper files to a fully digital, customized platform**
- Long-term partnership built on service quality and platform reliability
- Compliance managed proactively with full confidence in the system

---

## Key Themes

Liberty Disposal's story illustrates the difference between a *vendor* and a *partner*:
- They didn't just want software — they wanted a team behind them
- Switching to Foley revealed their previous provider's shortcomings immediately
- The combination of platform + service is what drives lasting satisfaction
