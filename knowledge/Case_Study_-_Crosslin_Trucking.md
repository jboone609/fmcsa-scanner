# Crosslin Trucking Builds a Scalable Safety Program with Foley

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/case-study-crosslin-trucking-and-foley](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/case-study-crosslin-trucking-and-foley)

---

## Company Overview

Crosslin Trucking is a third-generation, family-owned carrier based in Tennessee. The company recently transitioned from local freight operations to over-the-road (OTR) freight, significantly increasing its compliance complexity and regulatory exposure.

---

## Key Contact

**Tim Chelette** – Safety Manager  
*2024 TCA Professional Driver of the Year*

---

## The Challenge

- Transitioning from local to OTR freight meant navigating new DOT compliance requirements
- Brand new DOT number with a **New Entrant Audit** on the immediate horizon
- Tim had not worked in an office environment in 23 years — compliance software and digital file management were unfamiliar territory
- No existing digital safety program; needed to build from the ground up

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley provided a centralized, digital platform to manage:

- **Driver Qualification (DQ) Files** — organized and accessible
- **MVR (Motor Vehicle Records)** — integrated and automated
- **Background Checks** — streamlined pre-employment screening
- **DOT Drug & Alcohol Testing** — compliant program management

---

## Results

- Built Crosslin's **first-ever digital safety program**
- Audit-ready ahead of New Entrant Audit
- On track to **nearly double the fleet by 2026**
- Simplified onboarding and compliance management for a non-office background operator
