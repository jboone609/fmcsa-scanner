## 2. Avatar Fleet

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Specialty in Driver Safety, Training & Recruitment
- Customer Service
- Integrated compliance management
- Strong industry partnerships
- AI-driven tools to optimize hiring and compliance
- Pricing transparency

**Weaknesses**
- Limited brand recognition
- High pricing model
- Smaller client base
- Little international presence
- Dependence on transportation sector
- Does not offer drug & alcohol testing
- Limited FMCSA Clearinghouse support

**Opportunities**
- Target Small Market prospects
- Expansion into international markets — Mexico & Canada
- New Service Lines

**Threats**
- Rapid technological changes
- Prefer bundled solutions
- Regulatory compliance challenges

### 2.1 Summary

Avatar Fleet is a competitor for Foley's Mid-Market and Enterprise prospects. Their price point is high and Small Market Clients are less likely to work with them. Their tools for recruitment and driver safety training are a threat to Foley clients. Although, Avatar Fleet does not provide complete compliance experience for customers. Foley should continue outreach to prospects, especially those in need of drug and alcohol and Clearinghouse support.

---

## 3. DISA Global Solutions

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Wide range of compliance and background check services
- Integrations across various HRIS, ATS and recruiting software
- PHMSA dominance and contractor partnerships

**Weaknesses**
- Data Security Concerns
- Processes such as DQF Record Keeping are manual
- Driver Recruitment

**Opportunities**
- Integrations across various HRIS, ATS & recruiting software
- IFTA Support
- Increase occupational health support
- Increase Foley's presence at conferences

**Threats**
- Standing Integrations: Partner with other Foley competitors — TenStreet, Samba, and various HRIS & ATS software
- Network of clients and partnerships in the industry

### 3.1 Summary

DISA Global Solutions is Foley's biggest competitor for customers that need to prioritize drug testing and background check services. They have mobile and on-site drug testing options. Although, DISA supports clients of all sizes with multiple products to assist with driver compliance. For areas outside of their scope of services, they partner with others through integrations to supplement their weaker areas. By continuing to expand our products in the occupational health space, implementing integrations and increasing our marketing at industry events, Foley will be a strong competitor for prospects.

## 4. FleetDrive 360

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Vehicle Maintenance documentation & centralization
- Mobile App
- Modern UI
- Cloud Storage for documents
- Stronger partnerships
- Simple pricing

**Weaknesses**
- Limited brand recognition
- Lack of Automation
- Steep learning curve to software
- Challenging integrations
- Software often has issues or bugs

**Opportunities**
- Affiliate Program
- Non-regulated Drivers
- Artificial Intelligence

**Threats**
- Potential lack of resources
- Scalability up-market

### 4.1 Summary

FleetDrive 360 is a competitor for Small Market prospects but lacks industry recognition. It is unlikely Mid-Market or Enterprise clients would utilize FleetDrive 360 due to their software frequently having issues with coding bugs with limited integrations and automations. While their software offers a competitive suite of products, it lacks the quality demanded by industry. Foley should continue outreach to Small Market prospects, as we can offer a comprehensive service.

## 5. Fleet Safety

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Fuel tax & Permit services
- Truck Driver Training Simulator
- Hours of Service (HOS) Tracking
- Focus on SMB clients
- Personable service
- Consulting service

**Weaknesses**
- Smaller client base
- Lack of marketing
- Doesn't offer CSA scoring
- Small team
- No recruitment products

**Opportunities**
- Create additional SMB service options
- Provide tracking for vehicle maintenance
- Provide HOS tracking
- Increase marketing and support for SMB prospects

**Threats**
- Focus mostly on DOT Compliance
- High Costs
- Lack of vehicle maintenance logging
- New focus to adopt larger clients
- Have a point of contact for SMB Clients

### 5.1 Summary

Fleet Safety is a direct competitor for Small Market clients that provides a personalized approach to client's needs but does not have a comprehensive suite of products. Unlike Foley, they can help with Fuel Tax, Permits and tracking Hours of Service (HOS). Fleet Safety's scope is limited and will not support enterprise level clients due to its limitations in scalability, online visibility, and technology adoption. Foley should consider a new approach to Small Market clients to create a personalized experience or model a consulting approach to improve client satisfaction. By increasing market presence, particularly targeting small businesses, and integrating new technologies like HOS compliance and vehicle maintenance tracking will further strengthen Foley's position in the industry.

## 6. HireRight

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Operations in multiple countries
- Comprehensive background screening
- Integrates with various third party applications
- Up-to-date with latest regulations
- Platform is user-friendly

**Weaknesses**
- Customer Service complaints
- Limited product scope
- Processing times
- Billing issues

**Opportunities**
- International reach
- Higher client costs
- Integrations with HRIS systems

**Threats**
- Clearinghouse management
- DOT Compliance Expertise
- Electronic CCFs
- More global reach opportunities

### 6.1 Summary

HireRight is a competitor for clients primarily looking for background check services for clients and prospects of all markets. HireRight is a good fit for companies that do not need comprehensive compliance services but want to focus on expanding recruitment tools. HireRight is easily integrated into various ATS and HRIS systems and would be beneficial for clients that need to streamline their background check process. Foley should prioritize integrations with ATS and HRIS systems to become a comprehensive recruitment solution.

## 7. JJ Keller

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Encompass App — emphasis on ELD compliance but features integrations with their services
- API available to customers for custom integrations, as well as other dedicated Third-Party platforms
- Wide variety of training in digital and physical formats

**Weaknesses**
- Lack of onboarding solutions
- Encompass mobile app has inconsistent reviews and is only available on Android
- Inconsistent pricing; desirable features are restricted to highest pricing tier

**Opportunities**
- Continue expanding recruitment tools
- Competitive pricing

**Threats**
- Existing API solutions give a foundation for future integrations
- Possible expansion of Encompass app for Apple or other device support

### 7.1 Summary

JJ Keller is a direct competitor for Foley's Small Market, Mid-Market but not as many Enterprise clients. They offer a multitude of products for driver compliance but are not a leader for a particular area. JJ Keller meets clients' needs for a comprehensive platform with an inconsistent pricing model but lacks recruitment tools and uses an API for customer integrations. Foley should prioritize improving recruitment tools and establishing integrations.

## 9. TenStreet

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- DriverPulse (AI-infused app) that connects carriers with 340k drivers monthly
- Intelliapp allows for increased conversion rates
- JobStore (online Marketplace) posts to 20+ job boards with one click
- True Fuel improves fuel efficiency with True Fuel Score
- Truck Map shows parking availability along GPS route

**Weaknesses**
- High costs
- Background checks are done via integrations
- Complex interface
- Customer Service via dedicated emails
- API Setup lacks detail and technical documentation

**Opportunities**
- AI Infused App
- GPS Technology, Smart Navigation
- Rewards program
- Ability to upload photos, videos, police reports for secure storage and sharing

**Threats**
- Brand recognition among drivers
- Free Mobile App
- Carrier Network reach with DriverReach Acquisition
- Acquired driver communication provider TextLocate

### 9.1 Summary

TenStreet is the leading competitor for Enterprise clients that offers a comprehensive compliance solution. They are the closest competitor to Foley with similar processes. They can provide clients with recruiting tools and free app-based support on Androids and Apple products. Recruiting tools and platforms are known to be complex and cumbersome. Foley should continue focusing on expanding our product suite with additional features, implementing AI, adding integrations and developing an API while continuing to increase our presence in the industry.

## 10. Foley

### SWOT Analysis

**Strengths**
- Corporate account support with dedicated onboarding representative
- Background check automations
- Customer Service support
- No charge for inactive files
- Insurance partnerships with Keystone & Marsh
- Drug & Alcohol RTD & Follow-up process

**Weaknesses**
- Automation in Dash Onboard module
- Application not available in Spanish
- Small Market accounts have limited support
- Drug & Alcohol Module is limited

**Opportunities**
- Create vehicle maintenance tracking
- Use AI to generate bilingual applications & document support
- Develop integrations and API for HRIS & ATS systems

**Threats**
- No integrations with HRIS or ATS systems — more manual processes
- No automation in Onboarding module
- Mobile App
- No vehicle maintenance tracking

### 10.1 Summary

Foley is a leading competitor in the industry that offers a comprehensive compliance solution for clients. Currently, they do lack the ability to meet industry demand without HRIS and ATS integrations. Clients do receive customer service and customer success support without increasing costs and it is a significant strength compared to competitors. To meet Small Market clients, it will be beneficial to increase our client support and add bilingual application and document support with the use of AI prompts. Foley should continue to improve the current suite of products for recruitment and onboarding with automation. To be a stronger competitor for all market segments, it will be important to develop an open API with integrations for HRIS, ATS and other applicable systems, such as UltiPro, ICIMIS, and UKG to allow clients customization options.

## 11. Key Takeaways

For this project, it is important to note that not all competitors for Foley are included in the document. However, Foley, along with the competitors provided, are the industry leaders that are meeting client needs. Foley is one of the few competitors that provide a comprehensive compliance solution and is going in the right direction to continue to meet industry demands. By continuing to expand and develop new products while remaining people-focused, Foley will continue to beat the other competitors every time.
