# Conductor Experiences Winning Combination with Foley & Greenhouse Integration

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/greenhouse-conductor](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/greenhouse-conductor)

---

## Company Overview

Conductor is a technology company that used **Greenhouse Software** as their Applicant Tracking System (ATS). They needed a background check vendor that could integrate seamlessly with Greenhouse while also delivering a candidate experience that matched Conductor's company culture.

---

## The Challenge

- Existing background check vendor (one of the largest in the U.S., inherited from a former parent company) could not deliver a **candidate experience** aligned with Conductor's culture
- The incumbent vendor's size worked against them — impersonal, inflexible, and not a cultural fit
- Needed a solution that integrated directly with **Greenhouse ATS** without disrupting existing workflows

---

## The Foley Solution

- **Foley background check solution** integrated directly with Greenhouse Software
- Delivered a **"six-star" customer and candidate experience** that matched Conductor's brand values
- Preserved existing ATS workflows while enhancing the screening process

---

## Results

- Seamless **Greenhouse + Foley integration** — no disruption to existing hiring workflows
- Elevated candidate experience consistent with Conductor's company culture
- Replaced a large, impersonal incumbent with a service-first partner
- Improved confidence in background check quality and compliance
