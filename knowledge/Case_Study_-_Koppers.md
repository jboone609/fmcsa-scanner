# How Foley's eApplication Reduced Koppers' Time-to-Hire from Four Weeks to Two Days

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/koppers](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/koppers)

---

## Company Overview

Koppers is a large industrial company managing **350+ drivers** across multiple locations with varying position requirements. Their paper-based driver application process had become a significant bottleneck in recruitment and onboarding, costing weeks of hiring lead time.

---

## Key Contact

**Kathy Jones** – Corporate Fleet Safety Manager

---

## The Challenge

- **Paper-based driver application** was slowing recruitment to a halt
- Onboarding a new driver took approximately **four weeks** from application to road-ready
- Managing applications across multiple locations with different position requirements added complexity
- Paper process created inconsistencies and delays that made competitive hiring difficult

---

## The Foley Solution

- **Foley eApplication** — fully digital driver application replacing the paper process
- Standardized yet flexible — accommodates multiple locations with different role requirements
- Integrated with Foley's broader compliance and onboarding platform

---

## Results

- **Time-to-hire reduced from four weeks to as little as two days** (per foley.io) / one week (per foleyservices.com)
- Dramatically improved recruitment velocity across all locations
- Multi-location, multi-position hiring made manageable through one platform
- Corporate Fleet Safety Manager reports high satisfaction with the ease and flexibility
