# Avochato Reduces Hiring Risks & Bolsters Candidate Relationships with Foley

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/avochato](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/avochato)

---

## Company Overview

Avochato is a text messaging software provider that needed a modern, candidate-friendly approach to background screening. Their previous vendor was large, impersonal, and inconsistent with the company's culture and brand standards.

---

## The Challenge

- Existing background check vendor was one of the largest in the U.S. — but the candidate experience felt impersonal and outdated
- Process did not align with Avochato's company culture or brand
- Slow background check completion was creating hiring delays

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley replaced the legacy screening vendor with:

- **Candidate text messaging** — background check invitations and status updates delivered via SMS
- Modern, brand-aligned candidate experience
- **SOC 2 compliant** platform for security-conscious tech companies

---

## Results

- Background checks completed **76% faster** than email-initiated checks (text vs. email initiation)
- Improved candidate relationships and experience throughout the hiring process
- Reduced hiring risk with compliant, reliable screening
- SOC 2 compliance provided additional peace of mind for a tech-forward company
