## Company Overview

Elberta Logistics is a newer logistics company that started its Foley relationship with a focused need: **DOT Drug and Alcohol Testing**. From that initial entry point, the partnership has grown to encompass broader hiring and compliance process improvements.

---

## The Challenge

- New logistics company building compliance infrastructure from the ground up
- Initial focus: establishing a compliant **DOT Drug and Alcohol Testing program** from day one
- Hiring and compliance processes needed to be built efficiently and correctly from the start
- Needed a partner capable of growing with the business as compliance needs expanded

---

## The Foley Solution

- Entry point: **DOT Drug and Alcohol Testing program** — compliant program setup and management
- Expanded partnership to include broader **hiring process improvements**
- **Compliance infrastructure** built correctly from the outset, avoiding early-stage compliance failures common to new carriers

---

## Results

- Compliant DOT Drug and Alcohol Testing program established from day one
- **Improved hiring processes** — faster, more consistent, compliant candidate onboarding
- Compliance infrastructure properly built for a growing logistics operation
- Partnership positioned to scale with Elberta's business growth
