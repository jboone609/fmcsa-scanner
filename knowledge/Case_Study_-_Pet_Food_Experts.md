# Pet Food Experts is Scaling Their Fleet to 100+ Drivers with Foley's All-in-One Platform

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/pet-food-experts-scale-fleet-with-foley](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/pet-food-experts-scale-fleet-with-foley)

---

## Company Overview

Pet Food Experts is a family-owned distributor of pet food and supplies, managing a fleet of **95 drivers** and scaling toward 100+. The company has been growing through acquisitions and needed a compliance and fleet management platform that could keep pace with rapid expansion.

---

## The Challenge

- Managing a large, growing driver fleet with increasing compliance complexity
- Needed a cost-effective solution that went beyond basic compliance — full fleet management
- Onboarding acquired fleets quickly and consistently during growth-by-acquisition
- Administrative burden of proactive driver and compliance management at scale
- Risk of fees, penalties, and violations from reactive (rather than proactive) compliance management

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley's **all-in-one platform** provided:

- Comprehensive DOT compliance management for 95+ drivers
- **Proactive driver and compliance monitoring** with real-time alerts
- **Seamless acquisition onboarding** — new fleets integrated into the platform efficiently
- Cost-effective alternative to managing compliance, fleet, and HR tools separately

---

## Results

- **25% reduction in administrative tasks** — team time freed up for higher-value work
- **Real-time alerts** reduced fees and penalties from compliance lapses
- **Seamless onboarding** of acquired fleets during M&A growth
- Proactive compliance management — issues identified before they become violations
- Positioned to confidently scale to **100+ drivers**
