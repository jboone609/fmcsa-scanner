# Customer Problems

Foley customers consistently face two major challenges.

## Problem 1 – Hiring Speed

Organizations in safety-sensitive industries must fill roles quickly to maintain operations.

However hiring is slowed by:

- Compliance checks
- Credential verification
- Testing requirements
- Documentation processes

## Problem 2 – Regulatory Risk

Failure to maintain compliance creates major exposure including:

- DOT audit failures
- Insurance premium increases
- Fines or penalties
- Legal exposure
- Operational shutdowns

Foley reduces this risk by **automating and centralizing compliance workflows.**

# Sales Process

Foley does **not rely on proof of concepts.**

Deals typically follow a consultative process.

### Typical Motion

1. Initial discovery call
2. Additional discovery with stakeholders
3. Product demonstration
4. Deep dive sessions with Foley subject matter experts
5. Commercial negotiation
6. Contract signature and close

Success depends heavily on **high-quality discovery and stakeholder alignment early in the process.**

# Sales Methodology

Jeff uses **MEDDIC qualification** to evaluate and manage deals.

Each opportunity should be evaluated against:

## M – Metrics

What measurable business impact will Foley deliver?

## E – Economic Buyer

Who ultimately controls the budget?

## D – Decision Criteria

What must the solution accomplish?

## D – Decision Process

How will the organization evaluate and approve the purchase?

## I – Identify Pain

What specific business problem is the organization trying to solve?

## C – Champion

Who internally wants Foley to succeed?
