# FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse Statuses
*What They Mean for You & Your Drivers*

---

## FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse Statuses & the New Clearinghouse-II

Starting **November 18, 2024** (or sooner for some states), State Driver Licensing Agencies (SDLAs) will remove the commercial driving privileges from the driver's license of any individual with a "prohibited" FMCSA Clearinghouse status — until the driver completes the return-to-duty (RTD) process.

**Any drivers with unresolved FMCSA drug and/or alcohol Clearinghouse violations will lose or be denied the use of their CDLs or CLPs.**

Now is the time to ensure your drivers will not be impacted by this regulation by:

- Running compliant queries in the Clearinghouse
- Checking their status
- Understanding the meaning of each status
- Taking any required action

---

## Clearinghouse Consent Statuses & What They Mean

| Status | Definition & Required Action |
|---|---|
| **Pending Driver Consent** | Consent for **limited queries** can be received outside of the Clearinghouse via electronic or paper signature (must specify a time range). Consent for **full queries** must be received electronically within the Clearinghouse for every full query performed on each driver. |
| **Specific Consent Required** | This status appears when: (1) A limited/annual query has returned potential violations, requiring a full query to now be run, or (2) A potential change to the driver's status has been reported within 30 days of the last query. |
| **Consent Refused** | The driver declined to consent to a full query being run. If this is a current employee, they should be removed from safety-sensitive functions. |

---

## Clearinghouse Query & Driver Statuses & What They Mean

| Status | Definition & Required Action |
|---|---|
| **Driver Not Verified** | The state, license number, name, or date of birth on the driver's license do not match the state-documented information. Double-check the information on the driver's license to ensure it was entered in the Clearinghouse correctly. |
| **Cancelled** | The query was cancelled before any result was returned. |
| **Completed / Driver Not Prohibited** | The query did not report any record of DOT drug or alcohol violations. **The driver is cleared to continue safety-sensitive functions.** |
| **Completed / Driver Prohibited** | Violations exist that require the driver to be removed from safety-sensitive functions until a return-to-duty plan has been completed. **The driver is now at risk of losing their CDL/CLP privileges under the Clearinghouse-II regulation.** |
