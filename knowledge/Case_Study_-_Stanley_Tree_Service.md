# Stanley Tree Service Cuts Hiring Time in Half with Foley Dash

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/stanley-tree-service](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/stanley-tree-service)

---

## Company Overview

Stanley Tree Service is a tree care and removal company managing a fleet of DOT-regulated drivers. Prior to Foley, the company relied on paper-based processes for driver recordkeeping and onboarding.

---

## Key Contact

**Mikayla Taillon** – HR Coordinator

---

## The Challenge

- **Paper-based recordkeeping** — driver files stored across multiple locations, no centralized system
- **Slow onboarding** — approximately 2 weeks per applicant from hire to road-ready
- **Audit preparation stress** — files scattered, difficult to compile quickly when needed
- Paper CCFs (Chain of Custody Forms) for pre-employment drug testing added administrative burden

---

## The Foley Solution

**Foley Dash** — a fully digital compliance and driver management platform:

- Automated document tracking and deadline alerts
- Organized, centralized Driver Qualification (DQ) Files
- Digital pre-employment drug testing (eliminated paper CCFs)
- Audit-ready file management accessible from any location

---

## Results

- **Cut time-to-hire in half** — from ~2 weeks to ~1 week per applicant
- **Audits now seamless** — all documents accessible with a single click
- Eliminated paper-based processes and manual tracking
- Reduced administrative burden on HR staff

---

## Key Quote

> *"Being able to have all of our documents and driver files a click away made everything so much easier."*
> — Mikayla Taillon, HR Coordinator, Stanley Tree Service
