# Samoan Transportation Gets Operating Authority and Manages DOT Compliance with Foley

**Source:** [https://www.foley.io/case-studies/samoan](https://www.foley.io/case-studies/samoan)

---

## Company Overview

Samoan Transportation is a new entrant to the trucking industry. Starting a trucking carrier is statistically high-risk — **90% of new carriers fail within the first 12 months**. Samoan Transportation has beaten those odds, now operating for 3+ years and actively growing.

---

## The Challenge

- Brand new to the trucking industry with no prior compliance infrastructure
- Needed to obtain **Operating Authority** (MC/DOT number) to legally operate
- Navigating complex FMCSA requirements as a first-time carrier
- No experience managing DOT compliance digitally

---

## The Foley Solution

Foley supported Samoan Transportation from day one:

- **Operating Authority** — expert guidance through the FMCSA registration and authority process
- **DOT Compliance Management** — fully digital platform for driver files, testing, and monitoring
- **Complete compliance visibility** — real-time view into compliance scores and audit risk

---

## Results

- Successfully obtained Operating Authority with Foley's support
- Now **3+ years in business** — well past the critical 12-month failure window
- **Added a second driver** and is actively expanding the fleet
- Managing DOT compliance digitally with full visibility into compliance standing
